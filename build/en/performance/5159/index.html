Information about residency and showings www.pyhavaim.blogspot.com & www.saal.ee

Residency is supported by: Cultural Endowment of Estonia

In 2003 I graduated from Viljandi Cultural Academy as a lighting designer. For the last 13 years I have mostly made light designs for theatre performances in different Estonian theatres (9 years in Von Krahl Theatre) but also for dance performances and live music concerts. For the last years I have also made some light installations and that's the way my light works are heading for right now.
www.et.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oliver_Kulpsoo

During my residency I'm going to work with LED and filament. The future and the past of the light. Do they go together or not. What's the outcome when they do match and when they don't what's then?
